About

Have you ever tried to make plans to go camping in Wisconsin's wonderful state park system, only to realize that every single campsite in the state has been reserved? Unless someone cancels their reservation right while you are looking, you're out of luck.

This has happened to us many times, which is why we made this website. We do the checking for you, so you simply tell us the dates you would like to camp, the parks you would like to camp at, and we'll send you an email if one of those sites opens up.

Once you get the email, you will still need to go to the Wisconsin State Parks Reservation System and complete your reservation there. We just tell you when it's available.

Right now, when you submit a search, we assume you are looking for tent spots, non-ADA only. We are planning to expand this in the future, so stay tuned for updates!

We also allow for "Instascraping" of the Wisconsin Park System, where you can select the parks you are interested in and the dates you want, then click Instascrape to get realtime results on which of those parks have available campsites! Instascraping is a little bit fragile, so if you don't get a result the first time you click the button, try clicking it again.

Have any questions? Shoot us an email at campfinder@campfinder.me. Happy camping!
