About

Have you ever tried to make plans to go camping in Wisconsin's wonderful state park system, only to realize that every single campsite in the state has been reserved? If you're not making plans and reserving sites right when the availabilities open, you're usually not able to go camping at all, much less at a park near you. Unless someone cancels their reservation while you are looking and you are able to snag it, you're out of luck.

This happened to us many times, which is why we made this site. We do the checking for you, so you simply tell us the dates you would like to camp, the parks you would like to camp at, and we'll send you an email if one of those sites opens up.

It's important to note that we do not do any of the booking. Once you get the email, you will still need to go to the Wisconsin State Parks Reservation System and complete your reservation there. We just tell you when it's available.

Right now, when you submit a search, we assume you are looking for tent spots, non-ADA only. We are planning to expand this in the future, so stay tuned for updates!

Have any questions? Shoot us an email at wiparkscraper@gmail.com. Happy camping!
